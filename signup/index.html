developer+ is a powerful, extensible, 12-month infrastructure credit tier of cloud services that Rackspace makes available to the serious technologist who wants to go for a test drive. Unlike other offers out there, developer+ gives you the core of what you need to build a full, feature-rich application, plus it puts you in direct contact with Rackspace's developers to help you get your idea off the ground. It's the developer offering you'd expect from Fanatical Support®.

Ready for Development

developer+ allows you to choose the services you need to get building today. We made sure that everything our own developers would want in an offer made it into developer+. It's powerful, simple and - best of all - useful. You pay raw infrastructure prices, and you get complete access to offerings like ObjectRocket, MailGun and the upcoming OnMetal offering.

Extensible from Day 1

In addition to the credit, you'll get access to the same great no-cost features we offer to all our customers. Things like Cloud Monitoring, Orchestration, Cloud DNS and Cloud Networks are all included with developer+, just like they would be for any Rackspace customer. Let us know if you need help setting any of them up.

Fanatical Support® for Devs

It wouldn't be Rackspace if we didn't have your back. The support we offer to developers often needs to be deeper than what other customers ask for. Because of that, we want to give you the time you need to get your questions answered. That's why we put you directly in touch with smart, development-focused technologists just like you. Rackers are ready to help you make your plans a reality.

Pay as you consume, at competitive prices.

Just as you'd expect, you can consume all Rackspace Cloud services at market-competitive, infrastructure-only pricing for any usage beyond the infrastructure credit we provide.

Stay in touch!

developer+ is all about radical transparency, and that's a two-way street. We want to give you a product you'd actually want to use, and we want you to give us feedback about how you're using it.

A big part of this offer isn't just about letting people test-drive our products or helping incubate great ideas, it's also about getting feedback on how Rackspace can get better at serving developers. See, we actually think that our best innovations come from listening to our customers. So we'll ask you to provide us with honest, candid feedback about what's awesome and what's not. We're serious about taking you seriously.

The sky is the limit: we give you the core; the foundation for 12 months - you choose the best fit set of services to plug in and add to build your application!

If you need help or run into some issue, you get two support calls a month. Oh, and those go directly to a seasoned software developer at Rackspace just like you - someone who speaks your language and understands your issue.
